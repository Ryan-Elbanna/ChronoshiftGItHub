Travel Rules and Regulations

Travelers must abide by the rules that travel guides have.
Rules include:
- Stay on the path at all times during the trip.
- No shooting until instructed by the travel guides. (Applies to hunting expeditions).
- Follow and abide by travel rules issued by travel guides at all times. Travelers are instructed to follow the travel guides at all times.
Travelers are also required to have equipment that is provided by the company and must keep it on at all times.

Terms of Service

All travel rules and regulations are necessary and must be followed. All rules that are issued by the travel guides must be heavily followed, as consequences and penalties will be given out for any inappropriate act. All travelers must abide by the rules in order to be able to use our services.
